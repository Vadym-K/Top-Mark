What is an Equipment Lease?

1. An Equipment Lease is a financial instrument that can be customized in a variety of ways to allow a business to acquire the use of new equipment through an affordable monthly payment. An Equipment Lease gives a business immediate access to revenue generating equipment at a fraction of the cost of purchasing it. This allows the business to multiply the productive power of its available assets, increasing the company’s ability to produce profit for its owners. At the end of the initial lease term, the business has the option to take ownership of the leased asset or renew the lease and upgrade the equipment
